You are upgrading an existing fintech app UI.

Do NOT modify the main dashboard.

Instead, enhance the **right sidebar (or expandable side panel)** by adding a new intelligent component.

---

🎯 COMPONENT NAME:

👉 “Impact Feed”

---

🧠 PURPOSE:

This panel shows **real-time consequences of user actions**

It should feel like:
• a live AI assistant
• a subtle financial awareness system
• not a warning system

---

📍 PLACEMENT:

• Right side panel OR slide-out sidebar
• Scrollable feed
• Always accessible but non-intrusive

---

🌈 VISUAL STYLE (LIGHT + PREMIUM):

• Background:

* Soft white / light gradient

• Cards:

* Rounded, soft shadows
* Slight glass effect

• Accent colors:

* Green → positive impact
* Orange → caution
* Red → negative impact

---

📦 CARD STRUCTURE:

Each item in feed = one “impact insight”

---

1. HEADER (small):
   🧠 AI Insight

---

2. MAIN TEXT (important):
   “₹500 spent on food”

---

3. IMPACT LINE (highlight):
   “Delays your Goa trip by 2 days”

---

4. OPTIONAL TAGS:
   • “Spending Pattern”
   • “Goal Impact”
   • “Savings Drop”

---

5. TIME LABEL:
   • “Just now”
   • “2h ago”

---

---

🎬 INTERACTIONS:

• New item:

* slides in smoothly from top

• Hover:

* slight elevation + glow

• Click:

* expands to show more detail

---

🧠 SMART UI FEEL:

• Use friendly tone:

* “Small changes make big differences”
* “You’re slightly off track”

• Add variation:

* not all messages negative

Example:
• “You saved ₹200 today — great job”
• “You’re back on track this week”

---

📊 ADD SMALL VISUAL ELEMENTS:

Optional but powerful:
• mini progress bar shift
• subtle arrow (up/down)
• tiny timeline indicator

---

📱 MOBILE:

• Collapsible bottom sheet
• Swipe up to view insights

---

🚫 AVOID:

• harsh warnings
• too many red alerts
• cluttered feed
• long text

---

🏆 SUCCESS CRITERIA:

This should feel like:
❌ notification list → FAIL
✅ intelligent financial activity feed → SUCCESS

---

OUTPUT:

• Sidebar panel design
• Multiple card variations (positive, negative, neutral)
• Expanded state UI

---

FINAL GOAL:

Make users feel their financial life is being quietly monitored and guided in real time.
